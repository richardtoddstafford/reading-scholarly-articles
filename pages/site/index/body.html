About the tool

Reading a Scholarly Article is a research tool to help students understand the necessary components and overall structure of scholarly research papers.

Navigation

You can always click the home () icon to return here. Different research fields are available by using the dropdown menu in the navigation bar at the top of the screen. Each field as an overview and article page. The overview provides a brief introduction to the field, highlights of important concepts, and some scholarly examples. The article is an interactive sample scholarly article with components highlighted.
